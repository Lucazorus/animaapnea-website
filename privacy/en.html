Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Anima Apnea. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

Anima Apnea is developed and maintained by Lucas Massoni, an individual developer. By using the app, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Data We Collect

We collect the following information when you use Anima Apnea:

  • Authentication data: Your name, email address, and a unique user ID, collected through Firebase Authentication when you sign in with Apple or Google.
  • Training session data: Apnea hold times, breathing session results, and table session records that you generate while using the app.

We do not collect any analytics data, advertising identifiers, or tracking information. There are no ads in the app.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Account management: To create and maintain your user account so you can access the app across devices.
  • Data synchronization: To sync your training sessions across your devices so your progress is always up to date.
  • App functionality: To display your training history, statistics, and progress within the app.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase infrastructure. Firebase provides industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest.
  • Secure authentication protocols.
  • Access control rules to ensure only you can access your data.

While we take reasonable measures to protect your information, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We encourage you to use strong, unique passwords and keep your device secure.

Third-Party Services

Anima Apnea uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication (Google): Handles user sign-in via Apple and Google accounts. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase Firestore (Google): Stores and syncs your training data. Subject to Firebase's Privacy and Security documentation.

We do not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Deletion: You can request the deletion of your account and all associated data. You can delete your account directly within the app or by contacting us.
  • Export: You can request an export of your training data in a portable format.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at lucas.massoni.contact@gmail.com.

Children's Privacy

Anima Apnea is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe that a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can take steps to delete that information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
